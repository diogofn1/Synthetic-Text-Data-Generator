# Sample Recipe
Here's a simple recipe that showcases the versatility of our artisanal bread:

**Grilled Cheese and Tomato Sandwich**

Ingredients:

* 2 slices of Healthym's artisanal bread
* 1 tablespoon of butter
* 1 tomato, sliced
* 2 slices of cheese
* Fresh basil leaves, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

1. Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
3. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in the grill.
4. Top with a slice of cheese, a tomato slice, and a sprinkle of chopped basil (if using).
5. Place the second slice of bread, buttered side up, on top of the filling.
6. Grill until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
7. Serve hot and enjoy!
